A Biologically Grounded Closed-Loop Framework for Organoid Intelligence Simulation
Three-Factor STDP with reward delivered as 10 Hz theta current and penalty as 200 Hz noise current. No backpropagation, no shortcuts — every mechanism has a named biological counterpart.

UKH: A Three-Layer Hybrid Microelectrode Array Architecture
A Quantitative Coverage Model for Volumetric Recording of Brain Organoids · v2
Surface-only arrays record 0% of a 2 mm organoid's inner 80%. A three-layer design recovers total coverage to ~41% and renders cross-layer synchrony measurable for the first time.
doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20498735
